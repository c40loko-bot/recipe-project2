The Best Omelette Recipe Ever

Description:

This is a brief description of what you need to successfully make a omelette. I learned this recipe in 2013. Hopefully one day I will open a bar and grill restaurant and this recipe will be included.

Ingredient List:

  • Peppers
  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Sausage
  • Ham
  • Steak

Instructions:

  1. Fry the egg
  3. Bake the bacon, sausauge, and ham.
  4. Add bacon, sausage, and ham along with peppers onto the fried egg.
  5. Cook for 10 minutes while turning gently turning omelette.
  6. Let omelette cool and enjoy.
