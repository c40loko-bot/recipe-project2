The Best Fish Recipe Ever

Description:

This is a brief description of what you need to successfully cook fish. I learned this recipe in 2009. Hopefully one day I will open a seafood restaurant and this recipe will be included.

Ingredient List:

  • Fish(tilapia)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Seasoning salt
  • Mustard
  • Hot Sauce
  • Flour

Instructions:

  1. Season fish with seasoning salt and hot sauce.
  2. Add fish to deep fryer.
  3. Cook fish to 10 min or until browning.
  4. Let fish cool.
  5. Add mustard and enjoy.
