The Best Pizza Recipe Ever

Description:

This is a brief description of what you need to successfully make a pizza. I learned this recipe in 2011. Hopefully one day I will open a pizza restaurant and this recipe will be included.

Ingredient List:

  • Pepperoni
  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Parmesan
  • Marinara
  • Ragu

Instructions:

  1. Knead our dough.
  3. Spread tomato sauce onto dough.
  4. Add cheese on top of tomato sauce.
  5. Spread pepperonis on top of the pizza.
  6. Put pizza in oven for 20 minutes.
  7. Let pizza cool and enjoy.
